What is chus.css?

A light solution to my CSS skill issues as a backend developer, enabling me to build visually appealing websites with a unique touch of personal design flair, or at least, I believe so.

I took some ideas from water.css and simple.css.

Layout

The layout is based on a 600px width body that contains a header, main and footer. Body and main tag are centered with flexbox.
